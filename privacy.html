Privacy Policy

Three Kingdoms Duel — Last updated: June 11, 2026

Overview

Three Kingdoms Duel is a fully offline, single-player game. We do not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any personal information or any other data about you.

Data Collection

The app collects no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, contacts, location, etc.)
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind
  • No advertising — the game contains no ads
  • No network access — the game runs entirely on your device

Game Save Data

Your game progress (cards, gold, equipment, medals) is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device, except through Android's standard system backup if you have enabled it — that backup is managed by Google under your Google account settings, not by us.

Children

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, no data is collected from children or any other users.
